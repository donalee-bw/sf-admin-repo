## How It Works | Government Licensee Close Won (CW) Process
The Government Licensee Close Won (CW) Process in Salesforce ensures that all necessary information is captured when closing opportunities for government clients. The following are the required fields and checks based on the specific scenario.

### 1. Required Fields for Government Licensee with 1:1 Single-Site Onboarding (Stage: Closed Won)
When a Government Licensee opportunity reaches Closed Won and the Onboarding Path is &quot;1:1 Single-Site&quot;, the following fields must be completed:
OBS Owner
SaaS 1:1 Onboarding Date
Current Enrollment
Capacity
Contact
Related Payer Account
Preferred Language

### 2. Required Fields for Government Licensee with Billing Status &quot;Hard Opt Out&quot;
If the Billing Onboarding Status is set to Hard Opt Out, the following additional field must be completed:
Billing Dropout Reason

### 3. Required Fields for Government Licensee (Stage: Closed Won, Non-New Program or Site)
For Government Licensee opportunities that are Closed Won, but the onboarding is not for a brand-new program or site, these fields are required:
Related Payer Account
Preferred Language
Current Enrollment
Capacity
Contact
Center Type
OBC Call Scheduled Date
OBC Owner
Billing Status at Close Won
Target Billing Start Date
Number of Students Processing Tuition Online

### 4. School Activation &amp; Onboarding Tracking
These fields were introduced to assess whether the school intends to onboard and activate. In Government Licensee opportunities, the product is offered to schools or customers for free, so they may not always follow through with the onboarding process. This differs from non-government opportunities where customers provide credit card details with the intent to activate.
It is important to complete these fields to ensure that the onboarding team receives quality Closed Won opportunities, especially when tracking school activation:
Did you collect EIN/bank info? (Answer with &quot;Yes&quot; or &quot;Follow-up&quot;)
Did you add staff? (Required for non-micro schools, and both staff and students are required for micro-schools)
Did you add students? (Required for non-micro schools, and both staff and students are required for micro-schools)
Did you add parents? (Not required, but encouraged)

### Key Benefits of the Gov Lic CW Process
Complete Information: Ensures all key fields are filled based on the opportunity's context.
Streamlined Workflow: Automates data validation and reduces manual effort for the sales team.
